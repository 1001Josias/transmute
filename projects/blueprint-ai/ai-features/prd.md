# AI & Automation Features

## Objective

Leverage AI to automate project management tasks, improving estimation accuracy and reducing manual overhead.

## Context

Future Features roadmap item.

## Functional Requirements

1.  **Smart Estimates**: AI analyzes task complexity (and history) to suggest time/effort estimates.
2.  **Task Suggestions**: AI suggests subtasks based on high-level task descriptions or PRD content.
3.  **Auto-prioritization**: Suggest priority based on blockers and deadlines.

## Success Metrics

1.  User accepts > 50% of AI generated suggestions.
